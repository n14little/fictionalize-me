How It Works

Getting Started

How does Fictionalize Me work?

Simply journal about your experiences, and our AI technology transforms your entries into engaging narrative fiction. You maintain creative control while our AI helps with structure, pacing, and storytelling elements.

Do I need to be a professional writer?

Not at all! Our platform is designed for everyone. Whether you're a seasoned writer or just starting, our AI assists in crafting your story while maintaining your unique voice.

Writing Process

How long does it take to create a book?

The timeline varies based on your writing pace and goals. Many users complete their first draft within 2-3 months of regular journaling. Our AI helps streamline the process while keeping your story authentic.

Will my personal information remain private?

Absolutely! You have complete control over what details to fictionalize. Our AI helps transform personal experiences into creative fiction while maintaining privacy.

Publishing & Earnings

How do I publish my book?

Once your story is ready, we guide you through publishing on major platforms like Amazon and Apple Books. We handle the technical details while you maintain full creative control and rights to your work.

How much can I earn?

You keep up to 70% of your book's sales revenue. Earnings vary based on pricing and sales volume, but many authors find it a rewarding way to share their stories while earning extra income.
